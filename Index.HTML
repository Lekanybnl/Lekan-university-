About Lekan University

Our History

Founded in 2025 in the heart of Lagos, Lekan University was established to provide world-class education rooted in innovation, integrity, and excellence. We are committed to shaping future leaders.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To deliver transformative education that empowers students to solve real-world problems.

Vision: To be a leading African university recognized globally for academic excellence and research impact.

Leadership

  • Vice-Chancellor: Prof. Olalekan Adeyemi, PhD
  • Registrar: Dr. Aisha Bello
  • Dean of Students: Prof. Chukwuma Eze

Campus

Main Campus: Lekki Expressway, Lagos, Nigeria.
Modern facilities, smart classrooms, well-equipped labs, and beautiful green spaces.

Admissions

Admission Requirements

Minimum of 5 credits in SSCE including English & Mathematics. JAMB score and Post-UTME screening.
